NUMBERS
The game uses short suffixes so big numbers fit on screen:
KThousand  (103) MMillion  (106) BBillion  (109) TTrillion  (1012) QaQuadrillion  (1015) QiQuintillion  (1018) SxSextillion  (1021) SpSeptillion  (1024) OcOctillion  (1027) NoNonillion  (1030) DcDecillion  (1033) ViVigintillion  (1063) TgTrigintillion  (1093) QagQuadragintillion  (10123) QigQuinquagintillion  (10153) SxgSexagintillion  (10183) SpgSeptuagintillion  (10213) OcgOctogintillion  (10243) NogNonagintillion  (10273) CeCentillion  (10303)
The prefixes NdXxx / OdXxx / … are used for steps within each group (e.g. NdDc = 1036, OdDc = 1039). You can type 1k, 5m, 2.5b in any input field.
INCOME & UPGRADES
Your income ticks every second. Buy upgrades in the CLICK and AUTO tabs to increase it. Upgrades get progressively more expensive — each one costs ~1.35× the last.
PRESTIGE
Once you have enough money, you can prestige — this resets your balance and upgrades but grants you Skill Points to spend in the SKILL tab on permanent upgrades. The cost scales up each time.
HACKING
Buy the Hack Module to unlock attacks. Pick a target from the leaderboard — if the hack succeeds you steal 15% of their balance. They can counter with an Encryption Shield. You can upgrade hack speed, steal %, and defense in the HACK upgrades tab.
STOCK MARKET
Prices drift every 30 seconds. There's a hard supply cap of 5 trillion shares per asset — as supply runs dry, prices spike. A 3% spread is charged on every trade (buy high, sell slightly lower). Total portfolio value is shown at the bottom of the MARKET tab.
CASINO
All games charge a 5% house fee on every bet. The fee is taken before the round starts and goes to the server — it does not come back on a win.

Blackjack — standard rules. Blackjack pays 2.5×, win pays 2×. Pick a table that matches your balance.
Roulette — bet on red/black (1:1), dozens (2:1), zero (35:1), or a straight number (35:1).
Crash — the multiplier climbs 1% every 100ms from 1×. Reaching 2× is fairly likely — but the crash chance ramps up quickly after that, so holding on gets dangerous fast. Cash out before it crashes to win bet × multiplier. Survive all the way to 200× and you win the jackpot automatically.
POKER
Texas Hold'em for up to 6 players. A 5% rake is taken from each pot (capped at $1M per hand) and goes to the house.

Small blind / Big blind — forced bets posted by the two players left of the dealer before any cards are dealt. The big blind sets the minimum bet for the round. Example: SB $500, BB $1k means the minimum raise is $1k.

Min buy-in — the least you can sit down with. You join at this amount automatically when you create a room. Must be at least 1× the big blind.

Max buy-in — the most chips you can bring to the table. Must be at least the min buy-in.

Bet actions: Check (pass if no bet), Call (match the current bet), Raise (increase the bet), Fold (discard your hand). If you don't act within 30 seconds you are auto-folded.
TOKENS
Name tokens let you change your display name after the free first change. Earn them through gameplay.
LOGIN TOKEN
Your account is protected by a secret login token stored locally. Back it up from the SETTINGS tab — if you lose it and need help recovering your account, contact an admin.
